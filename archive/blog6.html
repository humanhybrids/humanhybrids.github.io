Who Carousels?! V.2.1

10/30/2019 1:15pm

Game Credits:

Programmer:
Kristie Cook
Artist:
Jon Dorais
Music:
Kevin Macleod

Who Carousels is a fun, side-scroller game where the player controls a giraffe who must navigate between pinatas on a carousel and spiders because they will totally kill you, bro! Keep track of your score to try to beat your friends who play! But be careful because the carousel moves faster and faster as the score gets higher!

As of today, Friday, October 25, 2019, the game is perfect. I thought if I said it online it would be true. But the game has some issues that will be revised. Currently, the start screen needs to have instructions added to it and the credits also added. On the programming side, I need to add a ceiling so the giraffe can avoid the abyss above. Also, I need to make the spiders easier to pass through, possibly by extending their height they move.

The game is almost ready to be published. After these changes are made, the mechanics will be solid. Currently, the giraffe hops well, and his rigid body falls nicely. The sounds also work well and are intentionally funny (a horse neighing sound that plays every single time the sprite hits a collider. So, it plays once to several times each time you die). In the alpha version, there were no spiders, no sound, and no start screen. It also stayed at a constant speed. Now the game increases speed as the score grows.
